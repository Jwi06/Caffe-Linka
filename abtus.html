Our Mission

At Caffè Linkà, our mission is to provide a delightful
café experience, offering high-quality food and drinks
for every occasion. We strive to create a space where
you can enjoy great food and beverages, all from the
comfort of your own home with fast and easy online
ordering.

Our Vision

We envision Caffe Linka as a place where convenience
meets quality. Our goal is to expand and reach more
people, creating  a seamless dining experience for
those who want to enjoy restaurant-quality meals
and coffee without leaving their homes.

Our Story

Caffe Linka was born out of our love for great coffee
and delicious meals. We wanted to create a café that
combines the  warmth of a cozy restaurant  with the
convenience of online ordering. What started as a
small café quickly grew into a passion project, and
now we’re here to share our culinary journey with
you. Whether you're ordering for a solo treat or
gathering with friends, we're here to make every
meal special.
